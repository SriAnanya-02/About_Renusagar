About Renusagar Power Plant

Our Story

Renusagar Power Plant, a unit of Hindalco Industries Limited
(a flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group), is a captive thermal power station located in Sonbhadra, Uttar Pradesh.

Established in 1965, the plant primarily serves Hindalco's aluminum smelting operations with an installed capacity of 840 MW,
Renusagar combining operational excellence with sustainable practices, featuring modern emission control systems
and water conservation measures.

Our History

  • 1965: Established as part of Hindalco's integrated aluminum facility
  • 1982: Major expansion doubling plant capacity
  • 1998: Implemented advanced emission control systems
  • 2007: Awarded "Best Performing Power Plant"
  • 2015: Launched community development initiatives
